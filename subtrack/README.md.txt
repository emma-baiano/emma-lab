# Subtrack

### Track your subs. Subtract the surprises.

**Subtrack** monitors your subscriptions and alerts you before charges hit so you stay ahead and in control.


## Features

- Track subscription cost, billing cycle and next charge date.
- Get notified when free trials are about to expire.
- Receive reminders for upcoming charges within 7 days.
- Built with Python, Markdown, and structured CSV.


## How It Works

1. Add your subscriptions to subscriptions.csv

2. Run reminder.py to check for upcoming charges

3. View reminders in your terminal
